WELCOME ELIMU TRACK

We are an independent website that tracks the funding received in Kenyan public Primary schools. We aim to deliver transparency in school funding programs and show how funds are utilised in the schools. Register with us!

Our History

The quality of education in Kenya is severely undermined by a critical lack of transparency and accountability in the disbursement of public funds to schools. To address this, Our team the GT Alphabits proposed the development of a public facing, transparent website for tracking the disbursement of all funds to primary schools in Kenya.

Our Mission

We believe that by creating a transparent, public website to track fund disbursement to primary schools in Kenya, we can reduce corruption and ensure more money reaches its intended purpose â€” improving educational resources for all Kenyan children.

Our Vision

The Vanessa Grant Trust (VGT)
Copyright Â©2025 Vanessa Grant Trust.

The Vanessa Grant Trust (VGT) brings educational opportunities to children and young adults in Rongai, Nakuru, in the Rift Valley in Kenya, approximately 200km north of Nairobi. In addition to infrastructural and professional development for staff, the Trust provides bursaries for children to attend a residential school for young people with disability, and for young women to attend a residential high school in Rongai, with further bursary support for promising graduates to attend university or college outside the area.
