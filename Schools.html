Overall Funding Shortfall (2020–2024)

A 2021 report by the Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission (EACC) found that only 45% of allocated funds for school infrastructure in a sample of counties reached the intended schools.

Source: EACC Report on Public Fund Management in the Education Sector, 2021

Visual: A simple pie chart showing 45% "Funds Received" and 55% "Funds Lost".

The government's core funding is not allocated to a list of specific schools, but rather is distributed per student to every single public secondary school in Nairobi.
Specific school by school disbursement data is treated as internal budgetary information by the Ministry of Education and is not typically released to the public.

School Disbursement Data

Empower citizens to compare allocated funds against actual school improvements, acting as a tool to hold local and national governments accountable for financial management.

By making financial flows visible, this solution directly tackles the root cause of fund misappropriation, ensuring that money reaches its intended destination to create safer, better resourced learning environments.
